about

Hi, I’m Natalie.

For more than 10 years I’ve worked as a news reporter, producer, anchor, writer, on-camera host, social media strategist, creative lead — and whatever else the project needs.

My work spans topics as serious as the explosion of QAnon during the pandemic and how the undocumented population of Flint was affected by the city’s water crisis, to explaining why the U.S. is so bad at taking vacations and the bizarre history of period products.

You can find my work at Jessica Yellin’s News Not Noise, ATTN:, Vice, The Obama Foundation, and more. Before journalism, I considered a career in museums/academia and interned at the Smithsonian Institution — a love of learning (and history) still sneaks into everything I make.

I’m a fluent Spanish-speaker and live in Los Angeles.
